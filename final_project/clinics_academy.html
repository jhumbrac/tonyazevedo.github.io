The Academy

A school of the sport that is more than just a clinic. The Tony Azevedo Academy aims to be an entire life experience that will change the way you play water polo. Learn unique international training drills used in the top water polo powerhouse countries such as Serbia and Hungary. The Academy will also include sessions on physical therapy, nutrition, mental preparation, networking with coaches, preparing and applying for college and staying at the top of your game in school.

The Winter Academy is held annually from December 27-30. 2015 will be at Segerstrom High School in Santa Ana, CA.
